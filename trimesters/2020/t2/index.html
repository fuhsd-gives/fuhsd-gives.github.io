2020 Trimester Two:
May -> August 31st

By Santa Clara Gives,
09/06/2020 1:15am
Before April, we accepted mostly in-person (in-kind) donations. A record of these could be found at this spreadsheet. However, we have since moved to an online platform (santaclaragives.org) to limit physical contact with donors. From now on, we will summarize our achievements, for every trimester, in a report--like this one! This way, we are hoping to encourage more donations by publishing every little thing we had accomplished with your kind donations.

So far, we’ve donated over a thousand masks and hygiene kits to the places that need it the most, like Stanford Children Hospital, Valley Medical Center, and Santa Clara’s Kaiser Permanente. As of now, we have purchased around 6,000 masks and another shipment of 20,000 masks are currently being shipped from China.


Our members delivering 3000 masks!


We spent a lot of time finding locations to donate to. We wanted to get resources where people needed it the most, and we wanted to do it fast. Hospitals have been in desperate need of PPE (personal protective equipment), so fundraising for masks and hygiene kits was a high priority for us. We practiced safe social distancing procedures by dropping off these materials.

With all of the fundraisers going on purchase equipment, we quickly realized that it was best for our essential services to decide where they need the money to go to. So, we researched which local non-profit organizations used their finances efficiently and the impact of their work. In the end, we decided on an online fundraiser for Second Harvest of Silicon Valley, VMC Foundation, and Opportunity Fund’s Small Business Relief Fund. We also added a competitive factor with our county’s high schools, because who doesn’t like a bit of competition?

We hope you join us in the fight against COVID-19, so we can help those that need help the most. Let’s do this together.
