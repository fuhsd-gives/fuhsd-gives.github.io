About Us

We, Santa Clara Gives, are students from all across the county. We realized that it can be so easy to feel alone and helpless in this unique time, but as a community, we can make a meaningful impact. We strive to support organizations and businesses in need of financial assistance due to the COVID-19 pandemic together. If you have any questions for us, feel free to email us at santaclaragives@gmail.com.
